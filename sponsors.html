Our Sponsors

Founding Sponsor

Building FORGE from Day One

Founding Sponsor

PODS

Positive Outreach Development Society

PODS is a Georgia-based organization committed to community development and supporting transformative programming for incarcerated individuals. As FORGE's founding sponsor, PODS has provided critical early support — believing in the vision of peer-led mentorship before the first session ever began. Their commitment to positive outreach and human development aligns directly with FORGE's mission to build mentors who change culture from the inside out.

Your Impact

What Sponsors Support

Every dollar invested in FORGE goes directly toward building a safer, more rehabilitative environment. Here is where sponsor support makes the biggest difference.

📚

Curriculum Materials

Printed workbooks, handouts, scenario cards, and instructional materials for each of the 108 sessions across three phases.

📝

Participant Journals & Handbooks

Personal reflection journals and participant handbooks that guide each person through the 36-week transformation process.

🎓

Facilitator Training

Training programs for staff and peer facilitators, including certification in trauma-informed care and motivational interviewing.

📊

Program Assessment Tools

Pre- and post-assessments, tracking systems, and evaluation instruments to measure participant growth and program outcomes.

🔬

Research & Evaluation

Partnerships with academic institutions to study FORGE's impact on violence reduction, institutional culture, and recidivism rates.

🌎

Expansion to Additional Facilities

Scaling the FORGE model to other institutions across Georgia and beyond, bringing peer mentorship to more incarcerated individuals.

About FORGE

FORGE is a peer-led, evidence-based mentorship program designed to build a safer, more stable dorm culture by training men to lead through service, accountability, and conflict reduction.

"To turn time served into time that serves others."
